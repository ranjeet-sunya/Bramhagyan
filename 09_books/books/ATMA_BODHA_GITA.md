# आत्मबोध गीता
# Atma-Bodha Gita — The Song of Self-Knowledge

## तुम कौन हो? — Who Are You?

> **"उद्धरेदात्मनात्मानं नात्मानमवसादयेत् ।
> आत्मैव ह्यात्मनो बन्धुरात्मैव रिपुरात्मनः ॥"**
>
> "Uddhared atmanatmanam natmanam avasadayet
> Atmaiva hyatmano bandhur atmaiva ripur atmanah"
>
> "Elevate yourself by yourself; do not degrade yourself.
> The Self alone is the friend of the self, and the Self alone is the enemy of the self."
>
> — Bhagavad Gita 6.5

# प्रस्तावना — Introduction

This is the song of remembering.

You have forgotten who you are.

Not your name. Not your job. Not your family.

You have forgotten your TRUE identity — your place in the cosmic body, your Dharma to the whole, your superpowers that lie dormant.

This book will remind you.

*On the shores of a dying river, a seeker sits in despair. The water is polluted, the fish are gone, the banks are crumbling. A wandering sage approaches.*

# अध्याय १ — प्रथम अध्याय
# Chapter 1: The Question

---

**शिष्य उवाच / Shishya Uvacha (The Seeker Said):**

> हे गुरो, संसारे किं मम स्थानम्?
> को'हं? किमर्थं जातो'स्मि?
> पृथिवी रुग्णा, जलं दूषितम्, वायुः विषाक्तः।
> किं कर्तव्यं मया? किं मम धर्मः?

*"O Teacher, what is my place in this world?
Who am I? Why was I born?
The Earth is sick, water poisoned, air toxic.
What should I do? What is my Dharma?"*

---

**गुरु उवाच / Guru Uvacha (The Teacher Said):**

॥ श्लोक १ ॥

> **त्वं पृथिव्याः कोशिका असि ।
> यथा देहे कोशिका तथा भूमौ त्वम् ॥१॥**

> "Tvam Prithivyah Koshika Asi
> Yatha Dehe Koshika Tatha Bhumau Tvam"

**Translation:**
*"You are a CELL of the Earth.
As a cell is to your body, so are you to the Earth."*

---

॥ श्लोक २ ॥

> **देहे यथा कोशिकानां कोटिशः सन्ति
> तथैव पृथिव्यां प्राणिनां कोटिशः वसन्ति ।
> एकस्य धर्मभ्रंशे सर्वस्य कष्टम् ॥२॥**

> "Dehe yatha koshikanam kotisah santi
> Tathaiva prithivyam praninam kotisah vasanti
> Ekasya dharma-bhramse sarvasya kashtam"

**Translation:**
*"As billions of cells exist in your body,
So billions of beings live on Earth.
When one forgets its Dharma, all suffer."*

---

# अध्याय २ — द्वितीय अध्याय
# Chapter 2: The Fractal Truth

---

**शिष्य उवाच / Shishya Uvacha:**

> कथं मया ज्ञातव्यं यत् अहं कोशिका इव?
> किं प्रमाणम् अस्य?

*"How shall I know that I am like a cell?
What is the proof of this?"*

---

**गुरु उवाच / Guru Uvacha:**

॥ श्लोक ३ ॥

> **यथा पिण्डे तथा ब्रह्माण्डे ।
> यथा अणौ तथा महति ।
> एकं प्रतिरूपं सर्वत्र वर्तते ॥३॥**

> "Yatha Pinde Tatha Brahmande
> Yatha Anau Tatha Mahati
> Ekam Pratirupam Sarvatra Vartate"

**Translation:**
*"As in the microcosm, so in the macrocosm.
As in the atom, so in the vast.
One pattern exists everywhere."*

---

॥ श्लोक ४ ॥

> **पश्य स्वदेहम् — कोशिकाः सन्ति ।
> पश्य कोशिकाम् — अवयवाः सन्ति ।
> पश्य पृथिवीम् — त्वं च अन्ये च सन्ति ।
> सर्वत्र समं प्रतिरूपम् ॥४॥**

> "Pashya Svadeham — Koshikah Santi
> Pashya Koshikam — Avayavah Santi
> Pashya Prithivim — Tvam cha Anye cha Santi
> Sarvatra Samam Pratirupam"

**Translation:**
*"Look at your body — cells exist within.
Look at a cell — organelles exist within.
Look at Earth — you and others exist upon her.
Everywhere, the same pattern."*

# अध्याय ३ — तृतीय अध्याय
# Chapter 3: The Dharma of Each Level

---

**शिष्य उवाच / Shishya Uvacha:**

> यदि अहं पृथिव्याः कोशिका, तर्हि किं मम धर्मः?
> कोशिकायाः धर्मः किम्?

*"If I am Earth's cell, then what is my Dharma?
What is a cell's Dharma?"*

---

**गुरु उवाच / Guru Uvacha:**

॥ श्लोक ५ ॥

> **कोशिकायाः धर्मः पञ्चविधः —
> जातौ स्मृतिः, संकेतश्रवणम्, आवश्यकं ग्रहणम्,
> सेवा कर्म, समये मृत्युः ॥५॥**

> "Koshikayah Dharmah Panchavidhah —
> Jatau Smritih, Sanketa-Shravanam, Avashyakam Grahanam,
> Seva Karma, Samaye Mrityuh"

**Translation:**
*"A cell's Dharma is fivefold —
Remember its identity, listen to signals, take only what's needed,
Serve its function, die when it's time."*

---

॥ श्लोक ६ ॥

> **तथैव मनुष्यस्य धर्मः पञ्चविधः —
> आत्मस्मृतिः, प्रकृतिश्रवणम्, मितभोगः,
> लोकसेवा, कालज्ञानम् ॥६॥**

> "Tathaiva Manushyasya Dharmah Panchavidhah —
> Atma-Smritih, Prakriti-Shravanam, Mita-Bhogah,
> Loka-Seva, Kala-Jnanam"

**Translation:**
*"Likewise, a human's Dharma is fivefold —
Remember the Self, listen to Nature, consume moderately,
Serve the world, know when to let go."*

---

| ३ | Take only needed nutrients | Consume only what's needed |
| ४ | Perform assigned function | Serve your unique role |

# अध्याय ४ — चतुर्थ अध्याय
# Chapter 4: When Dharma is Forgotten

---

**शिष्य उवाच / Shishya Uvacha:**

> किं भवति यदा कोशिका स्वधर्मं विस्मरति?

*"What happens when a cell forgets its Dharma?"*

---

**गुरु उवाच / Guru Uvacha:**

॥ श्लोक ७ ॥

> **यदा कोशिका स्वात्मानं विस्मरति
> तदा सा अर्बुदं भवति ।
> अनियन्त्रितं वर्धते, अधिकं भक्षयति,
> संकेतान् न शृणोति, मृत्युं नेच्छति ॥७॥**

> "Yada Koshika Svatmanam Vismarati
> Tada Sa Arbudam Bhavati
> Aniyantritam Vardhate, Adhikam Bhakshayati,
> Sanketan Na Shrinoti, Mrityum Necchati"

**Translation:**
*"When a cell forgets itself,
It becomes CANCER.
It grows without limit, consumes excessively,
Ignores signals, refuses to die."*

---

॥ श्लोक ८ ॥

> **यदा मनुष्यः स्वात्मानं विस्मरति
> तदा सः पृथिव्याः अर्बुदं भवति ।
> अनियन्त्रितं वर्धते, अधिकं भक्षयति,
> प्रकृतिसंकेतान् न शृणोति, परिवर्तनं नेच्छति ॥८॥**

> "Yada Manushyah Svatmanam Vismarati
> Tada Sah Prithivyah Arbudam Bhavati
> Aniyantritam Vardhate, Adhikam Bhakshayati,
> Prakriti-Sanketan Na Shrinoti, Parivartanam Necchati"

**Translation:**
*"When a human forgets itself,
It becomes Earth's CANCER.
It grows without limit, consumes excessively,
Ignores Nature's signals, refuses to change."*

# अध्याय ५ — पञ्चम अध्याय
# Chapter 5: The Human Superpower

---

**शिष्य उवाच / Shishya Uvacha:**

> किं विशेषं मनुष्ये? किमर्थं मनुष्यत्वं दुर्लभम्?

*"What is special about humans? Why is human birth rare?"*

---

**गुरु उवाच / Guru Uvacha:**

॥ श्लोक १० ॥

> **देहे यथा मूलकोशिका (Stem Cell) सर्वत्र गच्छति
> सर्वरूपं धारयितुं शक्नोति
> तथैव पृथिव्यां मनुष्यः ॥१०॥**

> "Dehe Yatha Mula-Koshika Sarvatra Gacchati
> Sarva-Rupam Dharayitum Shaknoti
> Tathaiva Prithivyam Manushyah"

**Translation:**
*"As the Stem Cell in the body can go anywhere
And can become any form,
So is the Human upon Earth."*

---

॥ श्लोक ११ ॥

> **मनुष्यस्य चत्वारि महासिद्धयः —
> गतिसिद्धिः, रूपसिद्धिः, विवेकसिद्धिः, कर्मसिद्धिः ॥११॥**

> "Manushyasya Chatvari Maha-Siddhayah —
> Gati-Siddhih, Rupa-Siddhih, Viveka-Siddhih, Karma-Siddhih"

**Translation:**
*"Humans have four great powers —
The power of MOVEMENT, the power of TRANSFORMATION,
The power of DISCRIMINATION, the power of KARMA CREATION."*

---

## The Four Superpowers

### १. गति-सिद्धि / Gati-Siddhi (Power of Movement)

॥ श्लोक १२ ॥

> **वृक्षः स्थिरः, पशुः सीमितः,
> मनुष्यः सर्वत्र गन्तुं शक्नोति ।
> भूमौ जले आकाशे च ॥१२॥**

> "Vrikshah Sthirah, Pashuh Simitah,
> Manushyah Sarvatra Gantum Shaknoti
> Bhumau Jale Akashe Cha"

*"The tree is fixed, the animal limited,
But the human can go ANYWHERE —
On land, in water, in sky."*

---

### २. रूप-सिद्धि / Rupa-Siddhi (Power of Transformation)

॥ श्लोक १३ ॥

> **मूलकोशिका यथा हृदयकोशिका वा यकृत्कोशिका वा भवितुं शक्नोति
> तथैव मनुष्यः कृषकः वा वैद्यः वा शिल्पी वा गुरुः वा भवितुं शक्नोति ॥१३॥**

> "Mula-Koshika Yatha Hridaya-Koshika Va Yakrit-Koshika Va Bhavitum Shaknoti
> Tathaiva Manushyah Krishakah Va Vaidyah Va Shilpi Va Guruh Va Bhavitum Shaknoti"

*"As a stem cell can become heart cell or liver cell,
So can a human become farmer, healer, craftsman, or teacher."*

---

### ३. विवेक-सिद्धि / Viveka-Siddhi (Power of Discrimination)

॥ श्लोक १४ ॥

> **पशुः सहजवृत्त्या चालितः,
> मनुष्यः विवेकेन धर्माधर्मौ विचिनोति ।
> एतद् एव मनुष्यत्वस्य महत्त्वम् ॥१४॥**

> "Pashuh Sahaja-Vrittya Chalitah,
> Manushyah Vivekena Dharma-Adharmau Vichinoti
> Etad Eva Manushyatvasya Mahattvam"

*"Animals are driven by instinct,
Humans discriminate between Dharma and Adharma.
This alone is the greatness of humanhood."*

---

### ४. कर्म-सिद्धि / Karma-Siddhi (Power of Karma Creation)

॥ श्लोक १५ ॥

> **अन्ये प्राणिनः प्रारब्धं भोगयन्ति केवलम्,
> मनुष्यः नवं कर्म सृजति मोक्षं च साधयति ।
> चतुरशीतिलक्षयोनिषु मनुष्ययोनिः एका एव मोक्षद्वारम् ॥१५॥**

> "Anye Praninah Prarabdham Bhogayanti Kevalam,
> Manushyah Navam Karma Srijati Moksham Cha Sadhayati
> Chaturashiti-Laksha-Yonishu Manushya-Yonih Eka Eva Moksha-Dvaram"

*"Other beings only exhaust their Prarabdha,
Humans CREATE new Karma and can achieve Moksha.
Among 84 lakh species, only human birth is the door to liberation."*

---

# अध्याय ६ — षष्ठ अध्याय
# Chapter 6: The Stem Cell Dharma

---

**शिष्य उवाच / Shishya Uvacha:**

> यदि मनुष्यः पृथिव्याः मूलकोशिका, तर्हि तस्य विशेषधर्मः कः?

*"If humans are Earth's stem cells, what is their special Dharma?"*

---

**गुरु उवाच / Guru Uvacha:**

॥ श्लोक १६ ॥

> **मूलकोशिका देहे कुत्रापि गत्वा क्षतं पूरयति,
> तथैव मनुष्यः पृथिव्यां कुत्रापि गत्वा धर्मं स्थापयति ॥१६॥**

> "Mula-Koshika Dehe Kutrapi Gatva Kshatam Purayati,
> Tathaiva Manushyah Prithivyam Kutrapi Gatva Dharmam Sthapayati"

*"As stem cells go anywhere in the body to heal wounds,
So humans go anywhere on Earth to establish Dharma."*

---

॥ श्लोक १७ ॥

> **मूलकोशिकायाः त्रिविधं कार्यम् —
> क्षतपूरणम्, पुनर्निर्माणम्, सन्तुलनम् ।
> तथैव मनुष्यस्य त्रिविधं कार्यम् —
> पर्यावरणरक्षणम्, जीवविविधतापुनःस्थापनम्, धर्मसन्तुलनम् ॥१७॥**

> "Mula-Koshikayah Trividham Karyam —
> Kshata-Puranam, Punarnirmanam, Santulanam
> Tathaiva Manushyasya Trividham Karyam —
> Paryavarana-Rakshanam, Jiva-Vividhata-Punah-Sthapanam, Dharma-Santulanam"

**Translation:**
*"Stem cells have threefold work —
Wound healing, regeneration, balance maintenance.
Likewise, humans have threefold work —
Protecting environment, restoring biodiversity, maintaining Dharmic balance."*

# अध्याय ७ — सप्तम अध्याय
# Chapter 7: The Path of Remembering

---

**शिष्य उवाच / Shishya Uvacha:**

> कथं स्मर्तव्यं यत् अहं पृथिव्याः कोशिका?
> कथं धर्मं प्राप्नुयाम्?

*"How shall I remember that I am Earth's cell?
How shall I attain my Dharma?"*

---

**गुरु उवाच / Guru Uvacha:**

॥ श्लोक १८ ॥

> **स्मृतिमार्गः षड्विधः —
> श्रवणम्, मननम्, निदिध्यासनम्,
> दर्शनम्, सेवनम्, समर्पणम् ॥१८॥**

> "Smriti-Margah Shad-Vidhah —
> Shravanam, Mananam, Nididhyasanam,
> Darshanam, Sevanam, Samarpanam"

**Translation:**
*"The path of remembering is sixfold —
HEARING, REFLECTING, MEDITATING,
SEEING, SERVING, SURRENDERING."*

---

### १. श्रवणम् / Shravanam (Hearing)

॥ श्लोक १९ ॥

> **प्रथमं शृणु — "त्वं पृथिव्याः कोशिका असि" ।
> पुनः पुनः शृणु यावत् हृदयं स्वीकरोति ॥१९॥**

*"First, HEAR — 'You are Earth's cell.'
Hear again and again until the heart accepts."*

---

### २. मननम् / Mananam (Reflecting)

॥ श्लोक २० ॥

> **ततः चिन्तय — यदि अहं कोशिका, तर्हि मम कर्म किम्?
> मम भोजनं किम्? मम मृत्युः किम्? ॥२०॥**

*"Then REFLECT — If I am a cell, what is my function?
What should I consume? When should I let go?"*

---

### ३. निदिध्यासनम् / Nididhyasanam (Meditating)

॥ श्लोक २१ ॥

> **ध्याने उपविश्य अनुभव — अहं पृथिव्याः अंशः ।
> मम श्वासः पृथिव्याः श्वासः ।
> मम रक्तं नदीनां जलम् ॥२१॥**

*"Sit in meditation and EXPERIENCE — I am Earth's part.
My breath is Earth's breath.
My blood is the water of rivers."*

---

### ४. दर्शनम् / Darshanam (Seeing)

॥ श्लोक २२ ॥

> **उद्घाटय नेत्रे पश्य — वृक्षाः मम भ्रातरः,
> नद्यः मम शिराः, पर्वताः मम अस्थीनि ।
> सर्वत्र स्वकुटुम्बं पश्य ॥२२॥**

*"Open your eyes and SEE — Trees are my brothers,
Rivers are my veins, Mountains are my bones.
See your family everywhere."*

---

### ५. सेवनम् / Sevanam (Serving)

॥ श्लोक २३ ॥

> **यथा मूलकोशिका देहं सेवते
> तथा त्वं पृथिवीं सेव ।
> वृक्षान् रोपय, जलं शोधय, प्राणिनः रक्ष ॥२३॥**

*"As stem cells serve the body,
So serve the Earth.
Plant trees, purify water, protect beings."*

---

### ६. समर्पणम् / Samarpanam (Surrendering)

॥ श्लोक २४ ॥

> **अन्ते सर्वं समर्पय — "अहं तव, त्वं मम" ।
> पृथिवी माता, अहं पुत्रः ।
> यद्यत् करोमि तत्तत् तवार्थम् ॥२४॥**

*"Finally, surrender all — 'I am yours, you are mine.'
Earth is mother, I am child.
Whatever I do, I do for you."*

# अध्याय ८ — अष्टम अध्याय
# Chapter 8: The Dharma in Daily Life

---

**शिष्य उवाच / Shishya Uvacha:**

> प्रतिदिनं कथं जीवनं यापयामि धर्मेण?

*"How shall I live each day in Dharma?"*

---

**गुरु उवाच / Guru Uvacha:**

॥ श्लोक २५ ॥

> **प्रातः उत्थाय स्मर — "अहं पृथिव्याः कोशिका" ।
> भोजने विचार — "किं मम देहाय आवश्यकम्, किं अधिकम्?" ।
> कर्मणि चिन्तय — "अयं कर्म पृथिवीं पोषयति वा पीडयति?" ।
> रात्रौ प्रश्न — "अद्य अहं धर्मेण जीवितवान् वा?" ॥२५॥**

**Translation:**
*"Upon waking, remember — 'I am Earth's cell.'
While eating, consider — 'What does my body need? What is excess?'
In work, think — 'Does this action nourish or harm Earth?'
At night, ask — 'Did I live in Dharma today?'"*

---

## Daily Checklist

### प्रातः / Morning

- [ ] Remember: "I am Earth's cell"
- [ ] Offer gratitude to the Sun (energy source)
- [ ] Drink water with awareness (it came from rivers)

### दिवा / Daytime

- [ ] Eat only what is needed (not more)
- [ ] Work for service, not just profit
- [ ] Reduce, reuse before consuming new

### सायम् / Evening

- [ ] Spend time with plants/nature
- [ ] Reflect on actions taken today
- [ ] Ask: "Did I heal or harm?"

### रात्रि / Night

- [ ] Apologize for any harm caused
- [ ] Set intention for tomorrow
- [ ] Sleep with gratitude for being alive

# अध्याय ९ — नवम अध्याय
# Chapter 9: The Great Truth

---

॥ श्लोक २६ ॥

> **न त्वं देहः, न त्वं मनः,
> न त्वं नाम, न त्वं कर्म ।
> त्वं असि चैतन्यं — पृथिव्याः चेतना ॥२६॥**

> "Na Tvam Dehah, Na Tvam Manah,
> Na Tvam Nama, Na Tvam Karma
> Tvam Asi Chaitanyam — Prithivyah Chetana"

**Translation:**
*"You are not the body, not the mind,
Not the name, not the work.
You ARE consciousness — Earth's awareness."*

---

॥ श्लोक २७ ॥

> **यथा कोशिकायां देहस्य चेतना वसति
> तथा त्वयि पृथिव्याः चेतना वसति ।
> त्वं पृथिवी, पृथिवी त्वम् ॥२७॥**

> "Yatha Koshikayam Dehasya Chetana Vasati
> Tatha Tvayi Prithivyah Chetana Vasati
> Tvam Prithivi, Prithivi Tvam"

**Translation:**
*"As the body's consciousness dwells in each cell,
So Earth's consciousness dwells in you.
You are Earth, Earth is you."*

---

॥ श्लोक २८ — महावाक्यम् ॥

> **"तत् त्वम् असि" — तत् पृथिवी, त्वम् असि ।
> "अहं ब्रह्मास्मि" — अहं विश्वस्य अंशः अस्मि ।
> "सर्वं खल्विदं ब्रह्म" — सर्वे प्राणिनः एकस्य देहस्य कोशिकाः ॥२८॥**

> "Tat Tvam Asi — Tat Prithivi, Tvam Asi
> Aham Brahmasmi — Aham Vishvasya Amshah Asmi
> Sarvam Khalvidam Brahma — Sarve Praninah Ekasya Dehasya Koshikah"

**Translation:**
*"'That You Are' — That Earth, You Are.
'I am Brahman' — I am part of the cosmos.
'All this is Brahman' — All beings are cells of one body."*

---

# अध्याय १० — दशम अध्याय
# Chapter 10: The Call to Action

---

**गुरु उवाच / Guru Uvacha:**

॥ श्लोक २९ ॥

> **उत्तिष्ठ! जागृहि!
> त्वं मूलकोशिका असि — पृथिवी त्वां आह्वयति ।
> क्षतानि पूरयितव्यानि, धर्मः स्थापयितव्यः ।
> तव विशेषशक्तयः सन्ति — उपयुङ्क्ष्व ताः ॥२९॥**

> "Uttishtha! Jagrihi!
> Tvam Mula-Koshika Asi — Prithivi Tvam Ahvayati
> Kshatani Purayitavyani, Dharmah Sthapayitavyah
> Tava Vishesha-Shaktayah Santi — Upayunkshva Tah"

**Translation:**
*"Arise! Awake!
You are a stem cell — Earth is calling you.
Wounds must be healed, Dharma must be established.
You have special powers — USE THEM."*

---

॥ श्लोक ३० ॥

> **गच्छ यत्र आवश्यकम् — तव गतिसिद्धिः ।
> भव यद् आवश्यकम् — तव रूपसिद्धिः ।
> जानीहि किं कर्तव्यम् — तव विवेकसिद्धिः ।
> कुरु यत् शुभम् — तव कर्मसिद्धिः ॥३०॥**

> "Gaccha Yatra Avashyakam — Tava Gati-Siddhih
> Bhava Yad Avashyakam — Tava Rupa-Siddhih
> Janihi Kim Kartavyam — Tava Viveka-Siddhih
> Kuru Yat Shubham — Tava Karma-Siddhih"

**Translation:**
*"GO where needed — that is your power of movement.
BECOME what is needed — that is your power of transformation.
KNOW what must be done — that is your power of discrimination.
DO what is good — that is your power of Karma creation."*

---

# समापन / Conclusion

---

॥ अन्तिम श्लोक ॥

> **अहं पृथिव्याः कोशिका ।
> मम धर्मः सेवा ।
> मम शक्तिः परिवर्तनम् ।
> मम लक्ष्यं धर्मस्थापना ।
> अहं स्मरामि — अहं कः अस्मि ॥**

> "Aham Prithivyah Koshika
> Mama Dharmah Seva
> Mama Shaktih Parivartanam
> Mama Lakshyam Dharma-Sthapana
> Aham Smarami — Aham Kah Asmi"

**Translation:**
*"I am Earth's cell.
My Dharma is service.
My power is transformation.
My goal is Dharma establishment.
I REMEMBER — who I am."*
