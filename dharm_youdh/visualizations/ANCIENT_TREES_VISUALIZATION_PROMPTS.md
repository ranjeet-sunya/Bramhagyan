- Add `--ar 16:9` for landscape
- Add `--quality 2` for detailed diagrams
- Add `--stylize 500` for artistic balance

**DALL-E:**
- More literal, descriptive prompts work best
- Specify "photorealistic" or "illustration" clearly
- Good at text integration

**STABLE DIFFUSION:**
- Great at specific art styles
- Specify "highly detailed" for diagrams
- Can struggle with text (use external tools)

- Excellent for medical/scientific diagrams
- Good at maintaining consistency
