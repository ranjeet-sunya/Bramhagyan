## WEEK 8: UNDERSTANDING SOIL

### Day 1-2: KNOW YOUR SOIL

```
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                                                              
   SOIL DIAGNOSIS:                                                             
                                                                              
   HEALTHY SOIL:                      SICK SOIL:                               
   ----------------------------       ----------------------------             
   Dark brown/black color             Light brown/gray color                  
   Crumbly texture                    Hard, compacted texture                 
   Earthy smell                       No smell or chemical smell              
   Earthworms present                 No earthworms                           
   Holds water well                   Water runs off or pools                 
   Supports diverse plants            Only weeds grow                         
                                                                              
   TEST YOUR LOCAL SOIL:                                                       
   1. Take a handful                                                          
   2. Squeeze it                                                              
   3. If it holds shape but crumbles = Good                                   
   4. If it turns to dust = Too dry, needs organic matter                     
   5. If it stays as hard ball = Too clay, needs aeration                     
                                                                              
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
```

**Practice Day 1-2:**
1. Find a plot of land you can work with
2. Do the soil test
3. Look for earthworms, insects
4. Assess: Is this soil healthy or sick?
